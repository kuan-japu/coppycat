# 🚀 CoppyCat v1.0.0 - Purr-fect Clipboard

Hello world! The first official release of **CoppyCat** is finally here! 🎉 
CoppyCat is a lightweight, funny, and blazing-fast Clipboard Manager specifically designed for macOS.

## ✨ What's hot in this release:
- 👻 **Stealth Background**: Sneakily captures all your copies without draining your battery.
- 🖼️ **Image Support**: Directly supports saving and thumbnail previewing of image files.
- ⚡ **Global Shortcut**: Summon the app instantly anywhere with `Cmd + Shift + C`.
- 🔍 **Parse Options**: Copy a block of text, click an option, and the app will auto-extract URLs or format messy JSON into clean code.
- 🎨 **"Glassy" Design (Blur UI)**: Gorgeous macOS-native experience that automatically adapts to Light/Dark mode.
- 💾 **Offline Storage**: Restart your machine worry-free; your last 80 clipboard items are safely stored locally.

## 📦 Quick Installation
1. Click on the `CoppyCat-1.0.0.dmg` file in the **Assets** section below to download.
2. Open the `.dmg` file and drag the **CoppyCat** icon into your `Applications` folder.
3. Open Launchpad and click on CoppyCat to start!
